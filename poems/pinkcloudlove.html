Pink Cloud Love

edgar cuevas

Pink cloud love creation
Is all you ever give me
Drowning in your sunlight
Never felt so goddamn right
Knowing it'll always turn out fine

My smile rises when you're around
And I just want to let you know

I'll never let you go
While I remain under your control
My heart beats fast
Whenever you say it'll last
Knowing you won't ever let go
Even if we leave Chicago

My heart keeps on bleeding
Yet it no longer has to remain hidden
Keeping me away from drowning
Out on the shallow end
It makes me smile knowing you're there
My anxiety fades when you're around
And I just want to let you know

I'll never let you go
While I remain under your control
My heart beats fast
Whenever you say it'll last
Knowing you won't ever let go
Even if we leave Chicago

Knowing there's certainty
With every smiling stare
Helps me finally cope
All this fear and anxiety
We're more than a casual flare
Knowing we'll never let go
