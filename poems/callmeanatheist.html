Call Me An Atheist

edgar cuevas

You asked me if I was fine
But I thought of a false response line
Because if I’d answer honestly
You would not want anything to do with me
So I just told you that I’d be okay

Lying is always easy
When you can’t explain why you’re sad
Because I’d go mad
Thinking about “what causes all these dark thoughts?”
I’m good with words
But I’m bad at explaining myself outside these records I record
But the world will never hear
How I’m good at contemplating suicide
But I’m bad at seeking the help I need
So I’ll just sit in the depths of hell
Knowing I’ll never be able to get out

No amount of therapy will ever fix me
These thoughts will never let me be free Even the most hardcore medication won’t make me forget of the time your love
Was taken away from me
Or the nights I cried until I fell into deep sleep
Hoping I’d never be woken

My grades are slipping this semester
Everyone says I’ll be remembered
As the next Bill Gates
But I won’t ever get to that point when I’m constantly having dates
With Miss Erray
She makes me feel like I won’t ever be able to breathe
But my heart senses that peace is so close
But in reality it’s a long trip away
You asked me if I’d be fine
But I’m not sure how long I can go writing these fake lines
I have this fake role I play everyday
I smile at everyone every time they look at me
But I go home crying
Because I can’t stand the anxiety of just living
And I keep promising
But not giving it my best effort
Because I’m scared to get better
Because when I go back to being happy
My words will no longer have a true meaning
My mind would be silent
With no voices screaming in this hollow head
My inner demons would stop asking me to end my life
So they could be set free

The silence would drive me to insanity
Because I would no longer be the person I am currently
I would miss the old me
Constantly wondering if people would notice this change in me
I find myself crying some nights
And dying sometimes seems like the best solution
I go to sleep every night hoping
That I will no longer be barely coping
Without a guiding light
Maybe tonight will be the night
That my life ends itself
Maybe tonight will be the night
I won’t wake up
And won’t have to live another tomorrow
Constantly trying to learn how to swim in this self-pitying sorrow
I stand isolated by these walls
While I see everyone around me breathing fresh air
I’d kill to have a fresh state of mind for just 5 minutes
A short amount of time
Where I have few moments of not thinking about suicide

It’s pathetic
Right?
Because so many people bow to a god
Praying that everything will be okay
But people claim god can’t love faggots
So call me an atheist

You asked me if I would be okay
I answered honestly
And then you decided to stay away
